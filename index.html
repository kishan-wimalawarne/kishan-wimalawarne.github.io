I am a postdoctoral research at Suzuki lab at the Department of Mathematical Informatics at the University of Tokyo. My supervisor is Prof. Taiji Suzuki.

I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Bio-Knowledge Engineering Research Laboratory at Kyoto University from April 2016 to March 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Hiroshi Mamitsuka

I finished my doctoral studied under the supervision of Prof. Masashi Sugiyama.

My research interests are deep learning (PINN, operator learning), tensors, learning theory, AI4Science, GreenAI/SustainableAI (AI4GreenAI,AI4SustainableAI).
